About


The Cult Online is a multiplayer game that contains at least 2 teams, the village and the cult. The Villager's objective is very simple, which is to succeed every quest. On the other hand, the cult must sabotage the quests by failing them. There are 5 quests in total. If 3 out of 5 quests are approved, the village win, otherwise, the cult wins.

There are special roles that may help both the village and the cult. One of it is the Seer that benefits every team. The Seer knows who the cultists are BUT, there is a catch! The Seer can't reveal themselves because if they do, at the end of the game, if the cultist did not manage to sabotage enough quests, the cult may guess the Seer. If they are correct, the Cult wins!

Some roles help to balance out the game. If the first quest was a success, the players will select those players to enter the quests for the rest of the game. This is where, special roles come in game. These roles, such as the Fool and Headhunter have special win conditions. For example, the Fool's only objective is to get in all quests or be voted out from the cultist (when the cult tries to guess the seer).
